## Setup your page

Learn how to start your page and deploy it to the public.

### Getting started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="globe" title="Go to the owner dashboard">
  In order to create your free MoreGyms page, you must go to the [owner dashboard](https://dashboard.moregyms.com/signup).

  </Accordion>
    <Accordion icon="right-to-bracket" title="Create your free account">
    Once you are on the [owner dashboard](https://dashboard.moregyms.com/signup), you will be asked to select the fitness you offer, an email and a password. You can ONLY CHOOSE ONE type of fitness offered. For the login credentials, make sure you are using an email that you check frequently and a password that is secure.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Deploy your page

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion icon="rocket" title="Save your changes">
On pages like [Gym Info](https://dashboard.moregyms.com/info), you need to click save so your work can be updated across our mobile and web application.
</Accordion>
<Accordion icon="apple" title="Install our mobile app">
In order to see how you page is reflected to users, [download the MoreGyms mobile app](https://moregyms.com/download) or click the "View Your Gym's Page" button, located on the navigation tab of your dashboard.
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
